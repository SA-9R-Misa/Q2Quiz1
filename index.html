East Asian Delicacies

Photo Country Description
Japan

Ramen

A dish originating from Japan which contains
fish or meat soup that contains it's broth. It is mixed
together with noodles and different kinds of vegetables.
China

Char Siu

A Chinese BBQ boneless pork with sticky and sweet red coating.
The name means "fork roast". It is usually served with rice or noodles.
In Mandarin, it is also known as "cha shao."
Korea

Kimchi

A sweet and spicy fermented cabbage dish that is a bit spicy.
Its ingredients can vary, but napa cabbage and Korean radish are
common bases. Kimchi also often includes green onions, ginger, and garlic.
Mongolia

Buuz

A national dish of Mongolian and is usually sold by street vendors.
They are small dumplings that are steamed and filled with mutton
or beef. It is traditionally eaten at Lunar New Years.
Taiwan

Bubble Tea

A refreshing drink from Taiwan ,typically a combination of milky tea
and chewy tapioca 'pearls'.Tourists look forward to this sweet and delicious drink.
The most famous bubble tea is from Chun Shui Tang.
